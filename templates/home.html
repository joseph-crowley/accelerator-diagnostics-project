Project Components

Detector System

Our state-of-the-art detector system is designed to monitor accelerator operations in real-time, ensuring the smooth functioning of advanced scientific equipment.

X-Ray Diagnostics

Harnessing the power of x-rays, we provide essential diagnostics for beam particles and electric fields, offering a comprehensive insight into accelerator operations.

LYSO Scintillator

Utilizing LYSO scintillators paired with Silicon Photomultipliers, we detect minute energies. LYSO even self-calibrates, enhancing the precision of our diagnostics.
